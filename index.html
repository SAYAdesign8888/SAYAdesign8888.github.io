Family Art Workshops

Classes for kids include pottery and Art Explorations (a variety of media will be used). The Museum also offers special seasonal family and children's workshops. Classes are offered for children ages 5-10 years. We offer a pottery class for teens.

Classes for adults include drawing, acrylic painting, and ceramics. Special workshops include printmaking, plein air painting, collage, and others. The Museum also occasionally hosts workshops by visiting artists.

Explore our exhibits in these free, one-session workshops led by teaching artists and designed to help grown-ups and kids enjoy art together! Families will get inspired in the galleries and then create their own artwork in the classroom studios. Most appropriate for kids ages 5-10, accompanied by an adult. Space is limited, and pre-registration is required. Click here to register.

Art lesson for kids

Can I use ideas from this website in my own Art classroom?
Yes! You have my permission to use the ideas on this website to create lessons in your own classroom. Some of my ideas are based on lessons I found online or from talking to other art teachers.
 
 

I am a new Art teacher... what should I do with my classes on the first week of school?？
On the first day of Art we do the rules, tour of the artroom, names and attendance, practice fire drill... then to keep it simple and open on the first day of Art I usually do the same project with all grades. This past year I read the story "The Dot", and the kids started with a simple "dot" on their paper and turned into an abstract design.
